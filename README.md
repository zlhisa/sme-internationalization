# AI Disclosure and SME Brand Authenticity

### Research Note Project | SS 2026

## Research Question

Does AI disclosure in brand communication reduce consumer trust through its negative effect on perceived brand authenticity?

## Hypothesis

- H1: AI-disclosed communication is perceived as less authentic than human-attributed communication.

## Theoretical Background

This project draws on signaling theory and brand authenticity literature. AI disclosure acts as a negative signal that weakens indexical authenticity cues — the perception of human effort and intent. SMEs rely on personal, relationship-oriented communication, making them potentially more vulnerable to authenticity penalties when AI use is disclosed (Schilke & Reimann, 2025; Eggers et al., 2013).

## Data

The analysis uses firm-level data from WRDS / Compustat Global.

## Empirical Strategy

ROA = beta0 + beta1 Firm Size + error
